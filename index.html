ABOUT BODH FALAK

Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Curious Minds

Bodh Falak was born from a simple question — why should children learn about their heritage from dry textbooks? Every card in our collection is a doorway. A doorway to the Ramayana's eternal values, to the Mahabharata's complex wisdom, to the towering intellects of India's Rishis and Rishikas.

Each card is two-sided: one face carries a beautiful illustration, the other carries meaning. Designed for homes, classrooms, bedtime stories, and gifting — Bodh Falak makes ancient Bharat feel alive again.
